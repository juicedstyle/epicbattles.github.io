Getting Started

Welcome to the world of Epic Battle Game! Here's a quick guide to get you started:

  1. Register: Create a new account by registering with your username, email, and password.
  2. Choose Your Class: Select one of the three classes: Human (Offense + Defense), Elf (Defense), or Orc (Offense).
  3. Profile: View and update your profile. Add a description to let others know more about you.
  4. Attack: Challenge other players to battles and earn points for winning.
  5. Guild: Join or create a guild to team up with other players.
  6. Statistics: Check the leaderboards and game statistics to see the top players and guilds.
